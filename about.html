The Label

Paper Vinyl Records is an independent label dedicated to the raw, the tactile, and the analog in a digital world.

Founded on the principle that music should be felt as much as it is heard, we focus on artists who push the boundaries of texture and atmosphere. From the first rip of a sleeve to the final crackle of the needle, we celebrate the physical journey of sound.

Our Vision

We believe in the "Paper Vinyl" aesthetic: something organic, slightly imperfect, but deeply intentional. Our roster is a collection of visionaries across lo-fi, electronic, and experimental genres.
