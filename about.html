The Story

Paper Vinyl Records is just an independent project focused on real music, textured art, and analog vibes.

Everything here is run by me. The goal is simple: support creators making dope lo-fi, electronic, and experimental sounds, and give them a home that cares about the physical aesthetic of music.

From torn paper artwork sleeves to the final needle crackle on a spinning record, Paper Vinyl celebrates the stuff you can actually feel.
